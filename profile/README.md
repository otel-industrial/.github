# OpenTelemetry Industrial

🏭 OpenTelemetry Industrial is a community-driven initiative focused on OpenTelemetry adoption in industrial, manufacturing, operational technology (OT), IoT, and legacy environments.

Our mission is to advance observability, cybersecurity resilience, and standards-based modernization across industrial systems through open technologies, knowledge sharing, and community collaboration.

## 🎯 Areas of Interest

* Industrial Observability
* Manufacturing Systems
* Operational Technology (OT)
* Industrial IoT (IIoT)
* Legacy System Modernization
* OpenTelemetry Integrations
* Industrial Protocols and Receivers
* Semantic Conventions
* Reference Architectures
* Cybersecurity and Asset Visibility

## 🤝 Community

We welcome contributors, practitioners, manufacturers, system integrators, software vendors, and OpenTelemetry community members interested in improving observability for industrial environments.

This organization serves as a place to:

* Share knowledge and implementation experiences
* Document existing projects and integrations
* Discuss industrial observability requirements
* Develop community resources and guidance
* Connect people working on similar challenges

Together, we can help make OpenTelemetry more accessible and effective for industrial systems and operational technology environments.
